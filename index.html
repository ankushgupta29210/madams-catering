About Us

Local Ingredients, World-Class Flavor

Based in the heart of Old Town Yellowknife, Madam's Catering Service brings the warmth of home-cooked meals with a refined culinary touch. We understand the unique logistics of the North.

Whether we are sourcing Arctic Char from Great Slave Lake or foraging for wild cranberries for our signature Bannock Bread Pudding, we pride ourselves on highlighting the incredible bounty of the Northwest Territories.
